Ice Sandwich Dessert

Ingredients

  1. 12 ice cream sandwiches
  2. 8 oz. frozen whipped topping
  3. 1/4 cup Smucker's Hot Fudge Topping
  4. 1/4 cup Smucker's Hot Caramel Flavored Topping
  5. 1/4 cup salted peanuts

Directions

  1. STEP 1
  2. Arrange 6 sandwiches in 9-inch square pan, cutting to fit. Spread with half of whipped topping. Spoon fudge topping on whipped topping. Drizzle 1/4 cup caramel topping over hot fudge.
  3. STEP 2
  4. Layer with remaining 6 ice cream sandwiches. Spread with remaining whipped topping. Freeze 3 hours or overnight. Remove from freezer 20 minutes before serving. Cut into squares.
  5. STEP 3
  6. Drizzle with hot caramel and hot fudge toppings before serving. Sprinkle with peanuts.

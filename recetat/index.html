Hot Fudge Brownie Dessert

Ingredients

  2. 36.8 oz. Pillsbury Family Size Chocolate Fudge Brownie Mix
  3. 1 cup Land O Lakes Butter
  4. 1 cup brewed coffee
  5. 4 eggs (
  6. 3/4 cup Fisher® Chef's Naturals® Chopped Pecans
  7. 14 oz. Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk
  8. 11.75 oz. hot fudge topping
  9. 8 oz. frozen whipped topping

Directions

  1. STEP 1
  2. Heat oven to 350°F. Spray 13x9-inch pan with Crisco® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray.
  3. STEP 2
  4. In large bowl, stir together brownie mixes, butter, coffee, eggs and pecans until well blended. Pour into pan.
  5. STEP 3
  6. Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted 2 inches from side of pan comes out almost clean.
  7. STEP 4
  8. In 1-quart saucepan, heat milk and fudge topping over medium-low heat about 5 minutes, stirring constantly, until hot (mixture must be hot). Cool brownies 5 minutes; poke several times with long-tined fork, wiping fork with paper towel if necessary. Pour hot fudge mixture over brownies. Cool 35 minutes before serving. Serve warm with whipped topping.

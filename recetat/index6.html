Julia Child’s Ratatouille

Julia Child’s ratatouille recipe is a delicate and robust vegetable stew with tomatoes, zucchini and eggplant and layered with fresh herbs and garlic.

Ingredients

  1. 1 lb eggplant/li>
  2. 31 lb zucchini
  3. 1 lb roma tomatoes blanched, peeled and seeded
  4. 2-3 garlic cloves smashed
  5. 1/2 onion sliced
  6. 2 bell peppers seeded and sliced in 1/2 in thick slices
  7. Small bunch of fresh parsley and/or basil chopped
  8. Olive oil for drizzling
  9. Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. STEP 1
  2. Begin by peeling the eggplant and slicing it in about 1/2in thick pieces and 3 in long.
  3. STEP 2
  4. Slice zucchini about same size as eggplant.
  5. STEP 3
  6. Place vegetables in a bowl and add 1tsp salt. Toss to coat and allow to sit for 30 minutes. Then drain and dry very well with a paper towel.Seperate the vegetables since they will be cooked seperately.
  7. STEP 4
  8. While the vegetables are sitting, prepare the tomatoes. Boil wter in a medium sized pot and once it is at a boil, add your tomatoes.
  9. STEP 5
  10. Allow to boil in the hot water for just about 10 seconds, this will allow the skin to come off easily.
  11. STEP 6
  12. Drop in a bowl fill with water and ice. This stops the cooking process.
  13. STEP 7
  14. Next, peel the tomato skin and discard. Cut the tomatoes in half and seed them. Then cut into strips. Reserve.
  15. STEP 8
  16. Heat a large skillet with olve oil and then sautee the eggplant first until lightly browned.
  17. STEP 9
  18. Remove the eggplant and sautee the zucchini until lightly browned and remove to a side dish.
  19. STEP 10
  20. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers and onions and cook slowly in the olive oil for about 10 minutes until tender but not browned. Add garlic and season with salt and pepper.
  21. STEP 11
  22. Add the sliced tomatoes on top of the peppers and onions. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and allow to cook for about 10 minutes until the tomato juices have rendered.
  23. STEP 12
  24. After about 10 minutes, uncover and raise the heat to reduce the casseroles juices, almost entirely.
  25. STEP 13
  26. Place 1/3 of the tomato mixture at the bottom of a casserole dish and sprinkle with fresh herbs. Layer half eggplant mixture on top, then tomato mixture, eggplant mixture, and the rest of the tomato mixture with more fresh herbs.
  27. STEP 14
  28. Cover casserold and allow to simmer for another 10 minutes while basting it’s juices. Taste for seasoning. Allow to simmer until juices have evaporated.
